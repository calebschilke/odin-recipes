Pizza Recipe

Description:

This receipe describes one of the best pizza recipes out there. Completely built from scratch and absolutely delicious!

Ingredients

  1. Pizza Dough
  2. Half a cup of pizza sauce
  3. 8 ounces fresh bocconcini mozzarella, sliced
  4. Half a cup sliced cherry tomatoes
  5. 10 basil leaves
  6. Pince red pepper flakes
  7. Extra-vigin olive oil, for drizzling

Steps

  • Preheat the oven to 500 F
  • Spread the pizza sauce on the dough
  • Top with the fresh mozzerella and tomatoes
  • Bake 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is brown
  • Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves and a pinch of red pepper flakes
  • Drizzle with olive oil and serve
